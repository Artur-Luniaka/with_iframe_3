Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters in every rotation

Information We Collect

When you play Rotate to Escape or use our website, we may collect certain information to enhance your gaming experience:

  • Game Progress: Your level completions, best times, and achievement unlocks
  • Device Information: Browser type, screen resolution, and performance metrics to optimize gameplay
  • Contact Information: Name and email when you reach out through our contact form
  • Usage Analytics: How you interact with different game mechanics and website features

How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us create a better puzzle-solving experience:

  • Saving your game progress and personal best scores
  • Optimizing game performance for your specific device
  • Responding to your questions and feedback
  • Developing new levels and game features based on player behavior
  • Ensuring fair play and preventing cheating

Information Sharing

We respect your privacy and do not sell your personal information. We may share data only in these specific circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and prevent fraud
  • With service providers who help us operate the game (under strict confidentiality agreements)

Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information, including encrypted data transmission and secure server storage. However, no online service can guarantee 100% security.

Your Rights

As a player, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data we have collected
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of non-essential data collection

Cookies and Local Storage

We use cookies and local storage to save your game progress and preferences. You can manage these through your browser settings, though this may affect game functionality.

Children's Privacy

Rotate to Escape is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.
