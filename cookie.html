Cookie Policy

Sweet treats for your browser, essential for your escape

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files that websites store on your device. In Rotate to Escape, cookies are like your personal escape toolkit - they remember your preferences, save your progress, and help us deliver the best possible puzzle-solving experience.

How We Use Cookies

Our cookies serve specific purposes to enhance your gameplay:

Essential Game Cookies

  • Progress Tracking: Saves your completed levels and current game state
  • Settings Memory: Remembers your preferred difficulty level and control settings
  • Session Management: Keeps you logged in during your gaming session
  • Performance Optimization: Stores graphics settings optimal for your device

Analytics Cookies

  • Gameplay Analytics: Helps us understand which levels are most challenging
  • Feature Usage: Shows us which game mechanics players use most
  • Performance Monitoring: Identifies technical issues to improve game stability
  • User Experience: Tracks navigation patterns to enhance website design

Functional Cookies

  • Theme Preferences: Remembers if you prefer dark or light mode
  • Language Settings: Stores your preferred language for game instructions
  • Accessibility Options: Saves any accessibility features you've enabled
  • Sound Preferences: Remembers your audio and music settings

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies:

  • Google Maps: For our contact page location display
  • Analytics Services: To understand how players interact with our game
  • Social Media: If you choose to share your achievements

Managing Your Cookies

You have full control over your cookie preferences:

Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete existing cookies
  • Block cookies from specific websites
  • Block all cookies (though this may affect game functionality)
  • Set preferences for different types of cookies

Game Settings

Within Rotate to Escape, you can:

  • Reset your game progress (clears progress cookies)
  • Opt out of analytics tracking
  • Manage functional preferences individually

Cookie Lifespan

Different cookies have different lifespans:

  • Session Cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Progress Cookies: Stored for up to 2 years to preserve your achievements
  • Preference Cookies: Stored for 1 year to remember your settings
  • Analytics Cookies: Stored for 6 months for statistical analysis

Updates to This Policy

As we add new features to Rotate to Escape, we may update our cookie usage. We'll notify you of any significant changes through the game interface or website announcements.
