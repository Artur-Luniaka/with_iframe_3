Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and improve your gaming experience. Think of them as your personal game save files!

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Game Cookies

Purpose: These cookies are necessary for the game to function properly.

What they do:

  • Save your game progress and high scores
  • Remember your control preferences
  • Maintain your session while playing
  • Store difficulty level settings

Duration: These cookies remain until you clear your browser data or they expire (typically 30 days).

Performance Enhancement Cookies

Purpose: Help us understand how players interact with the game to improve performance.

What they do:

  • Track which game features are most popular
  • Monitor game loading times and performance
  • Identify areas where players struggle
  • Collect anonymous gameplay statistics

Duration: These cookies typically last for 90 days.

Preference Cookies

Purpose: Remember your choices to personalize your experience.

What they do:

  • Remember your preferred graphics quality
  • Store audio and music volume settings
  • Save your chosen control scheme
  • Remember if you've seen tutorial messages

Duration: These cookies can last up to 1 year.

Local Storage & Session Storage

In addition to cookies, we use browser storage technologies to enhance your gaming experience:

Local Storage

Stores your game saves, achievement progress, and long-term preferences. This data persists until you manually clear it or uninstall the game.

Session Storage

Temporarily stores data during your current gaming session, such as current level progress and temporary settings. This data is automatically cleared when you close the browser tab.
